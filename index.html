ABOUT Fund

The future is in unity

Due to the Russian aggression, hundreds of thousands of Ukrainians were left homeless. The enemy continues to destroy the social infrastructure. Charity Fund “The Future Lies in Unity” unites the efforts of Ukrainian and international partners for reconstruction of the Ukrainian cities and communities.

OUR MISSION – is to help those people who are in need, and to create new social and economic opportunities for them.

Charity Fund “The Future Lies in Unity” has been rebuilding the destroyed social infrastructure in the regions affected by the aggression of the Russian Federation.

Assistance in reconstruction of Ukraine is the way to the European future, for which Ukrainians are fighting each day.

Ukrainians should be able to return to their homes. For this very reason, it is necessary to rebuild schools, hospitals, kindergartens, as well as housing and municipal utility facilities. This will make it possible to receive high-quality educational, medical, housing, and utility services.

We cannot stand aside, and will be rebuilding our country, because we strive to see Ukraine as a European state: an innovative, investment-attractive, and a country comfortable for life.

We should be rebuilding Ukraine now, bringing our victory closer.

“The Future Lies in Unity”

Each renovated hospital or school symbolizes the resilience of Ukrainians, faith in victory, and the futility of all efforts of the enemy. Our charitable foundation has united a powerful team of professionals from various fields who will work on rebuilding social infrastructure in various regions of Ukraine. Leading lawyers, economists, financiers, builders, manufacturers of building materials and products are with us. Together we are strong. We will unite because the future of Ukraine lies in unity.

Who we are?

The Fund`s team

Charity Fund “The Future Lies in Unity” was founded by Anton and Olena Lyashchenko. Anton is a practicing lawyer, attorney-at-law, and CEO of the law firm. Olena is a private entrepreneur who has been working in the agricultural sector for a long time.

Each of the fund’s projects is supported by a huge number of people: benefactors, volunteers, the fund’s team, which includes economists, financiers, builders, producers of building materials.

Our goals

Step by step, the Ukrainian defense forces are liberating Ukraine’s cities and villages from the Russian troops.

The fund will finance the reconstruction and renovation of:

  1. Educational institutions
  2. Healthcare institutions
  3. Centers for the provision of administrative services
  4. Critical infrastructure facilities (water supply, water disposal, and heat supply)
  5. Buildings to provide housing for internally displaced persons and persons who lost their homes as a result of hostilities caused by the armed aggression of the Russian Federation
